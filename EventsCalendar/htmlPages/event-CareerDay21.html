Exhibition February 18, 2025 • 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Career Day 21 – 21st Edition

University of Bahrain

Career Day 21 offers a unique opportunity for students and graduates to connect with leading companies and institutions from various sectors. The event aims to enhance employment and training opportunities, featuring informative workshops and direct engagement with potential employers.

Event Details

Date: February 18, 2025

Time: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM

Location: University of Bahrain

Admission: Open to all University of Bahrain students and alumni.​

Registration

Open to all University of Bahrain students and alumni.

Attendees are encouraged to bring copies of their resumes for on-the-spot applications.
