Tech Innovation Summit

Organized by Tech Club

Join industry leaders and peers for a day of innovation, networking, and inspiration at the annual Tech Innovation Summit. This premier event brings together thought leaders, entrepreneurs, and students to explore cutting-edge technologies and trends shaping our future.

The summit will feature keynote presentations, panel discussions, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities designed to foster collaboration and knowledge exchange.

Event Details

Date: October 15, 2023

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Location: Student Center, Grand Ballroom

Admission: Free for students, $25 for alumni and faculty

Agenda

9:00 AM - 9:30 AM: Registration and Breakfast

9:30 AM - 10:30 AM: Opening Keynote: "The Future of Technology" by Jane Smith, CTO of TechCorp

10:45 AM - 12:15 PM: Panel Discussion: "Emerging Technologies and Their Impact"

12:15 PM - 1:15 PM: Networking Lunch

1:30 PM - 3:00 PM: Breakout Sessions and Workshops

3:15 PM - 4:15 PM: Startup Showcase and Student Project Exhibition

4:30 PM - 5:00 PM: Closing Remarks and Raffle

Speakers

The event will feature prominent speakers from leading tech companies, startups, and academia, including representatives from Google, Microsoft, local tech startups, and faculty experts.

Registration

Space is limited, so early registration is encouraged. To reserve your spot, please register through the link below or contact the Tech Club at techclub@university.edu.
