Chinese Cultural Week Festival

University of Bahrain

Join us for the Chinese Cultural Week Festival, where you can enjoy artistic performances, workshops, and interactive exhibitions showcasing the rich heritage of Chinese culture. The event aims to promote cultural exchange and mutual understanding between communities.

Event Details

Date: April 13 – 17, 2025

Time: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM

Location: University of Bahrain – Cultural Plaza, Sakhir Campus

Admission: Open to all University of Bahrain students and alumni.​

Registration

Open to all University of Bahrain students and alumni.

Daily interactive activities include Chinese calligraphy classes and traditional costume experiences.
